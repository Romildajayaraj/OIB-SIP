ℹ️ About Temperature Scales

🌡 Celsius (°C): Based on the freezing point (0°C) and boiling point (100°C) of water at sea level.

🔥 Fahrenheit (°F): Commonly used in the USA. Water freezes at 32°F and boils at 212°F.

❄ Kelvin (K): Scientific scale used worldwide. Absolute zero (0 K) is the coldest possible temperature.

👉 This converter helps you switch easily between these units for daily, academic, and scientific use.
